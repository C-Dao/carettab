CaretTab has been updated to v3.0.0!

The biggest update yet!

Let's go over some important information and what's new.

Important:

This update changes the way settings are saved as well as changes a lot of features. Unfortunately this means your settings cannot be saved. You will need to apply your settings again. I apologize for the inconvienance this causes but I promise the update will be worth it!

The next screen will bring you through some basic options to get started.

What's new?

  • CaretTab has been recreated from scratch to improve performance and stability!
  • Clocks now have full customization! You can now set all settings on all clocks, give it a try!
  • Change the order of clocks and quick links.
  • More size options. Now most elements can be resized individually.
  • Wallpaper options! Use some provided textures or your own custom image.
  • Use any font installed on your machine!
  • Display your browser's bookmarks bar on the new tab page.
  • Lots more! take a look around and try things out.
